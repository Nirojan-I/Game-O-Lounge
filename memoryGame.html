How To Play:

Start the game by clicking the Start button, then flip a card. Then try to find another card that has the same image as the first. If you can't find a pair, the flipped cards will be flipped back with the face down. Try to remember these images as it becomes easier to find pairs the longer you play.

When you find a pair they are removed from the board. When you find all the pairs in this memory, you have completed the level.

Use the Restart button to suffle the cards or to restart the game. Your score is calculated acording to the number of moves you played to finish the game.
